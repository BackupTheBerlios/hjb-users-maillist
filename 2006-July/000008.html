> Unfortunately, it does - HJB assumes JMS 1.1 compliance, and the errors
> below are consistent with the creation of JMS1.0.2 objects that don't
> implement the JMS 1.1 interrfaces:
>
> javax.jms.ConnectionFactory  and javax.jms.Destination
>
> These do not exist prior to JMS1.1, hence you would get ClassCast
> Exceptions on registering pre-1.1 Topics, Queues and ConnectionFactorys.
